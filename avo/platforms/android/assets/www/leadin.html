The Juicy Details…

The truth about your “orange juice,” and fruit juice in general, is that it’s not real juice, even the 100% types. Fruits are seasonal. Even though they can still be bought year‐round, it doesn’t mean they’re going to taste good year‐round. Which is why fruits are processed—causing all fiber content to be removed— and then the subsequent liquid is stored in huge tanks, where they keep for a year. When it comes time to actually package the juice, ethyl butyrate is added, or as I like to call it, “Magical Packets of Flavor.” While it hasn’t been linked to health dangers, yet, wasn’t your juice supposed to be 100%? This packet gives juice the distinctive fruity flavor. Perhaps the most important thing for you to remeber is to drink real juice. Fruit juice, unless it was literally squeezed in front of your eyes, is not going to be healthy for you. Processed fruit juice is just another way for you to load up on sugar, without any of the useful nutrients.
